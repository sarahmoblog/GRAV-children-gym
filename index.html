About G R A V

Grav Club is a building that conducts a wide range of classes for all children and abilities. Our classes have been professionally designed to provide a strong motor foundation to develop strength, flexibility, balance and coordination. Thus providing excellent basics for picking up other sports. It will instil a sense of discipline and focus in your child. Above all, our highly professional coaches will ensure that your child has lots of fun at the same time.

Join Us at G R A V

What you can Expect

Children should grow while having fun. We ensure a balance of optimal learning and immerse enjoyment
